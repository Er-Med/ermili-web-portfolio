FAQ

Answers to your questions

Clear and helpful answers to all your inquiries.

What kind of websites do you build?

I design and develop modern websites for businesses that want to build trust, attract more customers, and grow online. Whether you need a company website, landing page, or custom web application, every project is tailored to your business.

How long does a project usually take?

The timeline depends on the project's complexity. Most business websites are completed within 2–6 weeks, while larger custom solutions may take longer. You'll receive a clear timeline before development begins.

Will my website be optimized for SEO?

Yes. Every website is built with SEO best practices, fast loading speeds, responsive design, and clean code to help improve your visibility on search engines and deliver a better user experience.

Can I update the website myself?

Absolutely. If your website includes a content management system (CMS), you'll be able to edit text, images, blog posts, and other content without needing any technical skills.

What happens after my website goes live?

Launching your website is just the beginning. You'll receive everything you need to manage it confidently, and if you prefer ongoing support, I offer maintenance, updates, performance improvements, and technical assistance whenever you need it.
